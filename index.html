About Me

Who is Mr.Boombox?

I'm a passionate sound designer and programmer with over 8 years of experience creating immersive audio experiences for games, applications, and interactive media.

My journey began in music production, but I quickly fell in love with the technical aspects of sound design and audio programming. This unique combination allows me to bridge the gap between creative vision and technical implementation.

When I'm not crafting soundscapes or writing code, you can find me experimenting with modular synthesizers, field recording in unusual locations, or contributing to open-source audio projects.
